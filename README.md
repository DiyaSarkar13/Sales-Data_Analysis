## ðŸŽ¯ Business Requirements Implemented

The dashboard was designed to address the following analytical objectives:

1. Top & Bottom 5 products by:
   - Sales
   - Profit
   - Quantity Sold

2. Sales trend analysis across:
   - Daily
   - Monthly
   - Quarterly
   - Annual levels

3. Relationship analysis between Sales and Profit.

4. Period comparison:
   - Compare Sales, Profit, and Quantity between any two user-selected time ranges.

5. Average discount analysis by promotion category.

6. Total number of orders.

7. Detailed order-level view including:
   - Sales
   - Net Sales
   - Profit
   - Discount
   - Units Sold  
   (Fully filterable by Product, Date, Customer ID, and Promotion Category)

8. Geographic sales distribution by city.
